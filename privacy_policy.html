SnapNote Privacy Policy

Thank you for using SnapNote.

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains what information SnapNote collects, how it is used, and your choices regarding this information.

Information We Collect

SnapNote does not collect or transmit any personal or sensitive user data.

The extension only stores the following data:

  • Notes you write
  • Video timestamps
  • Video title and URL
  • Screenshot of the current YouTube frame (captured locally)

All of this data is stored locally on your browser using Chromeâ€™s local storage.

How We Use Your Data

SnapNote uses this data solely to provide its core functionality:

  • Save timestamped notes while watching YouTube
  • Allow you to review saved snapshots and notes later

No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Data Security

Because all information is stored locally, you have complete control. Uninstalling the extension or clearing your browser's data will permanently remove your saved notes.

Third-Party Services

SnapNote does not integrate with or use any third-party services that track or collect user data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a new effective date.
